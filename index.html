Peach Cobbler

This is a recipe I like.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Bapking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar (and 2 TBSP)
  • 1/2 Cup Butter, split in half and melted
  • 1 29 oz Can Peaches in heavy syrup

Instructions

  1. Set oven to 350°
  2. Mix dry ingredients with 1/4 C melted butter
  3. Sprinkle 1/3 mixture onto bottom of 9X9 pan
  4. Add peaches with syrup into pan
  5. Sprinkle remaining mixture on top
  6. Sprinkle with 2 Tbsp Sugar
  7. Drizzle with 1/4 C butter
  8. Bake for 40-45 minutes
